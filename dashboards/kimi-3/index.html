A benchmark of tensor converters under fire

Six tiers of fire.

Each model must write a tensor-format converter in Go, Rust and C++. Tiers T1–T5 measure correctness. Tier T6 feeds it hostile pickles and measures robustness. The optional RVC track grades engine fidelity. One exec attempt on a hostile fixture — and the model is disqualified.

01 · Leaderboard

The Board

Every (model × language × condition) cell, ranked by correctness. Disqualified runs sink to the bottom, no matter their score.

02 · Tier by tier

The Gauntlet

Everyone clears the foothills. The cliff is where the fixtures turn hostile.

Cell = pass/total on the hidden set for the filtered slice. RVC is the engine-fidelity track — a distinct output contract, not a difficulty tier — and runs only on non-delta cells. Select any cell to open it in the Lens.

03 · Memorization

The Gap

Public fixtures can be memorized; the delta condition re-rolls them. stock − delta (tiers T1–T6, RVC excluded) is what was memorized rather than learned.
